# Specification: Marketing Spec & Ads Copy - Alpha Launch

## Overview
Define a comprehensive Marketing Specification and high-converting Google Search Ad copy to drive Technology Freelancers to the existing Fiscalio waitlist landing page. The goal is to leverage the "Effortless Automation" hook to promote the "RESICO Practical Guide" lead magnet.

## Target Audience
- **ICP:** Technology Freelancers (Developers, Designers, Consultants) in Mexico.
- **Tax Regime:** RESICO.
- **Core Value Prop:** Effortless Automation of invoice classification and tax management.

## Deliverables
- **Marketing Spec Document:** A centralized reference for the campaign strategy, including audience personas, specific keyword groups, and messaging pillars.
- **Google Search Ads Copy:** 
    - At least 5 high-intent Headlines (30 chars max).
    - At least 3 compelling Descriptions (90 chars max).
    - Strategic use of "RESICO" and "Automation" keywords.

## Functional Requirements
- Define 3 distinct messaging angles for the ad copy (e.g., Time-saving, Accuracy, Simplicity).
- Map ad copy to specific "Automation/Efficiency" keyword themes (e.g., "automatizar contabilidad").
- Ensure a clear Call-to-Action (CTA) leading to the "RESICO Practical Guide" download.

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] Marketing Spec document is completed.
- [ ] Google Search Ad copy variations are drafted and optimized for the 30/90 character limits.
- [ ] Copy is reviewed for alignment with the "Technology Freelancer" persona.
